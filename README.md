### 🌍 [Nigeria ART Coverage Dashboard](https://github.com/Tower-king/Nigeria-ART-Coverage-Dashboard)
**Python · Streamlit · Pandas · Altair · UNAIDS Data**

Interactive public health dashboard analysing adolescent HIV ART coverage across 100+ countries.
- Nigeria ranked **43rd globally** at 77% overall coverage
- Identified female-male disparity — attributed to PMTCT programming and male health-seeking behaviour gaps
- Filterable by indicator, age group, sex, year, and UNICEF region
